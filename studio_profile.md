## 2. Brand Story & Philosophy

### Why "unSAME"?

"unSAME" is a self-coined word born from an excess of creative energy and the ambition to become the weirdest game creators around. The core mission: make games that are "not the same" as what already exists.

Inspired by nostalgia for the early 2000s indie game explosion — when something new and unexpected appeared every day. Guiding principle: "If you want to play something different, you have to make something different."

**CN brand voice (copy-ready):**
> 想要玩到不一样的游戏，就要先做出不一样的游戏。专做怪游戏，unSAME is Fun!

### What kind of games do we make?

**Keywords:** Weird / Heartfelt / Something you haven't played before.

**Approach:** Bold experimentation in unusual mechanics, unconventional controls, and emotional storytelling.

## 4. Content Creation Rules

| Dimension | Rule |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tone** | Humorous, energetic, proudly weird. Never generic marketing speak. |
| **Vocabulary (EN)** | Use: "weird", "different", "unexpected", "unSAME". Avoid: "exciting new game", "don't miss out". |
| **Vocabulary (CN)** | Use: 整活、怪游戏、不一样、前所未见 |
| **Visual direction** | Emphasise experimental and unconventional. Lead with mechanics, not just aesthetics. |
| **Key messages** | Highlight "never-seen-before" experience; cite real awards and milestones; always convey Fun. |
| **Priority rule** | In-game design details (Level 1) > pitch-deck summaries (Level 2). KB content overrides conversation context. |
